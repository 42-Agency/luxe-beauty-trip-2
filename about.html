Our Story

Two Friends. One Discovery. A Better Way to Travel.

Luxe Beauty Trip started the way most good ideas do — not in a boardroom, but in a conversation between two friends who couldn't stop talking about Korea.

Diana & Sophia — Seoul, Korea

How It Started

Diana and Sophia met through a shared fascination with Korean culture — the food, the skincare rituals, the way Korean women approach beauty as something to invest in rather than just maintain. What started as a friendship built on late-night conversations about K-dramas and fermented skincare eventually turned into something more intentional: an actual trip to Seoul.

Both in their 40s, they had been spending real money on aesthetic treatments at home — and getting real results, but not without the sting of real prices. A single HIFU session. A round of skin boosters. A laser treatment. The bills added up fast, and the waitlists were long. Korea had been coming up in their research for months. So they went.

What They Found

What they found in Seoul wasn't what they expected. The clinics weren't budget operations. They were exceptional — cutting-edge technology, doctors who had performed these procedures thousands of times, and treatments that simply weren't available back home at any price. Rejuran Healer. Juvelook. Exosome therapy. Things their local providers had never heard of or couldn't legally offer.

The results were real. And the cost was a fraction of what they'd been paying at home. But what struck them most wasn't the price — it was how straightforward the whole experience was once you knew where to go and what to ask for. The barrier wasn't the treatments themselves. It was the knowledge gap.

Why Luxe Beauty Trip Exists

That knowledge gap is exactly what Luxe Beauty Trip was built to close. Every question Diana and Sophia had to figure out on their own — which clinics to trust, which treatments to combine, how to time everything around the trip, where to stay, what to do between appointments — is now what they do for their clients.

They're not running a clinic directory or a booking platform. They're two women who did the research, found the best clinics, navigated the language, got the results, and came back wanting to share all of it. Every itinerary they build is the trip they wish someone had planned for them the first time.

"We spent years paying full price for less. Korea changed what we thought was possible — and we wanted every woman who felt the same way to be able to access it." — Diana & Sophia
