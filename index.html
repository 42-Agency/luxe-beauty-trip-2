Why Korea?

The same treatments. Better results. A fraction of the price.

A fraction of the price

Treatments that cost $800–1,500 at a top med spa in New York, LA, or Miami typically run $200–500 in Seoul. Korea's highly competitive aesthetic market keeps quality exceptional and pricing honest.

Treatments not yet available at home

Rejuran, Juvelook, exosome therapy, and other treatments used daily in Korean clinics are not FDA-approved and simply aren't available at US med spas — at any price. Coming to Korea opens up options your provider back home can't offer.

Technology ahead of the curve

Korea receives new aesthetic devices and treatment innovations before most of the Western world. The clinics we work with invest continuously — you're accessing what's current, not what arrived five years ago.

Volume means precision

Seoul's aesthetic clinics treat in a volume that most Western clinics never reach. These doctors perform the same procedures daily, with a level of repetition that builds a precision that's genuinely hard to find elsewhere.
