Six Days of K-Beauty in Seoul

Seoul has some of the most advanced aesthetic clinics in the world, doctors who train at a level most markets never see, and treatments you simply cannot get at home.

We have built a six day trip that takes care of everything — the clinic, the culture, the dinners, the moments in between. You show up. We handle the rest.

This trip is limited to 12 women. Here is what six days in Seoul looks like.

The Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival and Welcome

Arrive at Incheon International Airport and transfer to your hotel in central Seoul. Check in, get settled, and meet the group over a welcome dinner. Your first taste of the city and the women you will be spending the week with.
Day 2

Culture Day

Gyeongbokgung Palace, the narrow lanes of Bukchon Hanok Village, Insadong's art market, and Namsan Tower. The day ends with a traditional Korean group dinner, a proper hansik spread to introduce you to Korean cuisine done properly.
Day 3

Clinic Day

The day you came for. Morning consultations at our pre-vetted Gangnam clinic with a licensed Korean dermatologist, followed by your personalised treatment session. Skin boosters, laser toning, lifting treatments, or whatever your skin needs. All treatments performed by licensed Korean doctors.
Day 4

Recovery and Shopping

Sleep in. Explore Garosu-gil or Apgujeong at your own pace. Browse Shinsegae or spend the afternoon lost in a beauty store. This day is yours, structured around recovery from Day 3 and whatever Seoul has left on your list.
Day 5

Beauty Experiences and Farewell

A complimentary personal colour analysis session, the legendary 13-step Korean hair treatment spa, or an afternoon at Myeongdong. The group comes together one last time for a farewell dinner before the final morning.
Day 6

Departure

Morning checkout and transfer to Incheon. You leave with glowing skin, a carry-on full of Korean skincare, and probably a group chat that will never go quiet.
