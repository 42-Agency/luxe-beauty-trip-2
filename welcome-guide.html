Who We Are

Why Korea

Why Women Are Travelling to Seoul for Beauty

Korea is not a trend. It is the global standard for aesthetic medicine and skincare innovation, and North American women are finally catching on.

🏥

Doctors Only. Always.

In Korea, all aesthetic procedures are performed by licensed medical doctors. Not nurses, not aestheticians. Every injection, laser, and energy device treatment is done by a physician trained specifically in aesthetic medicine.

⚡

Technology You Cannot Get at Home

Korean clinics use devices that have not yet reached North America. Shurink Universe, Sylfirm X, Juvelook, Oligio, and Rejuran (the Korean original) are available in Seoul years ahead of Western markets.

💰

40 to 60% Less Expensive

The same treatment that costs $3,000 in New York or Toronto can be done in Seoul by a specialist, in a world-class clinic, for a fraction of the price. Many clients cover the cost of their flights with savings on treatments alone.

🌸

A Complete Experience

Seoul is extraordinary. Ancient palaces, bustling markets, rooftop hot springs, colour analysis studios, and the best skincare shopping on earth. The treatments are the reason you come. The city is why you will want to return.

🩺

Clinical Rigour at Every Level

Korean aesthetic clinics operate at hospital-grade standards. Protocols, aftercare, and follow-up are thorough and well-documented. Our RN co-founder personally reviews every clinic on our list for safety, hygiene standards, and treatment quality before we ever send a client through their doors.

Sample Itinerary

What a Luxe Beauty Trip Looks Like

Below is a sample overview of a 6-day Seoul retreat. Your exact itinerary is shaped around your treatment plan, your goals, and your pace.

D1
Arrival and Welcome
Cultural

Private airport transfer to your hotel in Gangnam. Check-in, freshen up, and join us for an orientation dinner in Apgujeong. We walk you through your personalised treatment schedule, answer every question, and set you up for the week ahead.

D2
First Treatment Day
Treatment

Morning clinic consultation. Your doctor reviews your skin in person, confirms your treatment plan, and begins your first session. Common Day 2 treatments include Rejuran, skin boosters, or laser toning. Afternoon free to explore Garosu-gil or rest at the hotel.

D3
Cultural Immersion Day
Cultural

Rest your skin and explore the city. Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bukchon Hanok Village, Insadong market. Traditional Korean lunch. Optional: personal colour analysis studio session to discover your seasonal palette. This day is about Seoul, not procedures.

D4
Second Treatment and Shopping
Treatment Cultural

Morning clinic appointment for your second round of treatments, which may include HIFU, Sylfirm X, or thread lift depending on your plan. Afternoon in Myeongdong or the Shinsegae Department Store beauty hall, where our team guides you through the best skincare buys to take home.

D5
Wellness and Spa Day
Wellness

A restorative day. Traditional jjimjilbang experience at one of Seoul's iconic Korean bathhouses, or rooftop mineral hot springs at Starfield. Optional: 13-step Korean hair treatment spa in the afternoon. An early evening to pack, reflect, and celebrate your results.

D6
Departure
Departure

Private transfer to Incheon Airport. Aftercare kit and skincare instructions provided before you board. We remain reachable for any post-trip questions in the weeks that follow. You leave Seoul rested, renewed, and already planning your return.

* Treatments are booked and priced separately from the trip package. Your personalised treatment plan is confirmed during your intake consultation.

Our Services

Everything We Handle for You

From the moment you sign on as a client, we are your single point of contact for everything. Here is what that means in practice.

Travel Planning

Accommodation recommendations, transfer coordination, and a day-by-day itinerary built around your travel dates and pacing preferences. We make sure the logistics never get in the way of the experience.

Clinic Introductions

Treatment Booking

Concierge Support

Restaurant reservations, shopping guidance, cultural recommendations, translation assistance, and on-the-ground problem solving. We are your local expert even though we are not always physically with you.

Working Together

What We Each Commit To

A great trip is built on clarity. Here is exactly what you can count on from us, and what we ask of you in return.

What You Can Expect From Us

  • A response to all messages within 2 business days during your planning window. Discovery and planning calls are scheduled on set days so please allow for this when reaching out.
  • A personalised treatment plan built around your skin goals, health history, and budget, reviewed by Diana, our Registered Nurse, before anything is confirmed.
  • All clinic communications handled in Korean on your behalf. You will never need to navigate language barriers on your own.
  • Flight and hotel bookings handled directly by Diana as a licensed travel agent, for clients on the Concierge Package. No third parties, no markups you cannot see.
  • A complete pre-trip briefing covering your appointment schedule, clinic addresses, what to bring, pre-treatment prep, and aftercare notes.
  • WhatsApp support during your trip, Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm Korea Standard Time. We are reachable for clinic questions, logistics, and anything unexpected.
  • Post-trip follow-up within one week of your return to check in on your results, answer aftercare questions, and ensure you are happy.
  • Full transparency around scope. You will always be informed before any additional charges apply and nothing is billed without your written confirmation.

What We Ask of You

  • Complete your client intake form within 7 days of signing. This covers your skin history, health information, treatment goals, and travel preferences. Without it, we cannot build your plan.
  • Respond to planning messages within 3 business days. Planning timelines are tight and delays on your end create delays across your entire trip preparation.
  • Disclose your full health history accurately. This is not optional. Incomplete information creates real safety risks during aesthetic treatments and we cannot be responsible for outcomes when we have not been given accurate information.
  • Work exclusively with Luxe Beauty Trip for the planning of this trip. We invest significant time researching and coordinating on your behalf and ask for the same commitment in return.
  • Arrive on time to all clinic appointments. Korean clinics operate on strict schedules. Late arrivals may result in a shortened or cancelled session with no refund.
  • Notify us of any changes to your plans as early as possible. Late changes can trigger supplier cancellation fees which become your responsibility.
  • Follow all pre and post-treatment instructions from your doctor and from Diana. Recovery guidelines exist for your safety and ignoring them affects your results.
  • Treat this as a partnership. We do our best work when clients are responsive, honest, and trust the process we have built.
