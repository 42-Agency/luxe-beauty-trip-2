Day by Day

Arrive at Incheon International Airport and transfer to your hotel in central Seoul. Check in, get settled, and meet the group over a welcome dinner — your first taste of the city's energy and the women you'll be spending the week with.

Arrival Welcome Dinner

A full day exploring the best of Seoul: Gyeongbokgung Palace, the narrow lanes of Bukchon Hanok Village, Insadong's art market, and Namsan Tower. End the day with a group traditional Korean dinner — a proper hansik spread, your introduction to Korean cuisine done properly.

Gyeongbokgung Palace Bukchon Hanok Village Traditional Korean Dinner

The day you came for. Morning consultations at our pre-vetted Gangnam clinic with a licensed Korean dermatologist, followed by your personalised treatment session — skin boosters, laser toning, lifting treatments, or whatever your skin needs. All treatments performed by licensed Korean doctors. Clinic fees are paid directly to the clinic on the day.

Doctor Consultation Aesthetic Treatments Gangnam Clinic

Sleep in. Explore Garosu-gil or Apgujeong at your own pace. Browse Shinsegae or spend the afternoon lost in a beauty store. This day is yours — structured around recovery from Day 3 and whatever Seoul has left on your list.

Free Day Garosu-gil Shopping

Optional add-on: 13-step Korean hair treatment spa — bookable through Luxe Beauty Trip at additional cost.

A morning at a traditional jjimjilbang — the full Korean bathhouse experience. Afternoon personal colour analysis session to discover your seasonal palette. Evening: a farewell group dinner, the last night together before everyone heads home.

Jjimjilbang Colour Analysis Farewell Dinner

A slow farewell breakfast before a final sweep through Myeongdong for last-minute skincare hauls. Then airport transfer. You leave with better skin, a carry-on full of K-beauty products, and a group chat that will not go quiet any time soon.

Myeongdong Airport Transfer Departure
