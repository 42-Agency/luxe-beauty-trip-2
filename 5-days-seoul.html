Day by Day

Land in Seoul, check into your hotel, and breathe. Today is yours — no agenda, no clinic. Get oriented, explore your neighborhood, and recover from the flight. A welcome dinner to ease you in.

Seoul Arrival Welcome Dinner

Morning at the clinic — the doctor assesses your skin in person, walks through the treatment options we've discussed, and confirms exactly what's right for you. You leave with a clear plan and a clear cost. The rest of the day is wide open: beauty districts, street food, the city's energy.

Doctor Consultation Seoul Exploration
