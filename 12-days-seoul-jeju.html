About This Journey

Twelve days gives your skin the time it needs to respond — and gives you the space to truly experience two very different sides of Korea. Seoul is electric, efficient, and world-class for aesthetic treatments. Jeju is the counterpoint: volcanic, slow, and quietly restorative. Before you travel, we listen to your concerns and share treatment suggestions with estimated costs so you arrive with a clear picture. The doctor confirms everything in person on Day 2.

Day by Day

Seoul Days 1–7

Arrive in Seoul, check in, and do nothing that's on any agenda. Today is for recovering from the flight, getting your bearings, and arriving at dinner feeling human. A welcome dinner to start the trip properly.

Seoul Arrival Welcome Dinner

Morning at the clinic — the doctor assesses your skin, walks through the treatment options we've discussed, and confirms exactly what's right for you. You leave with a clear plan and a clear cost. The rest of the day is free: Seoul's beauty districts, its street food culture, its pace.

Doctor Consultation Seoul Exploration

A recovery day and a slow one. Gyeongbokgung Palace in the morning, the stone-walled lanes of Bukchon Hanok Village in the afternoon. Seoul's oldest neighborhoods at their own pace.

Gyeongbokgung Palace Bukchon Hanok Village

Morning at the clinic — your specialist continues the plan. Afternoon free to explore whatever part of Seoul calls to you: modern, traditional, or somewhere in between.

Clinic Appointment Seoul at Leisure

A full cultural immersion day — no clinics, no agenda. Record your own K-pop demo at a studio experience or book a Korean makeup class with a professional artist. Seoul at its most fun.

K-pop Studio Experience Korean Makeup Class

A final treatment session in Seoul. Afternoon at the Han River — a slow picnic, the city skyline, locals cycling past. A ritual that every Seoul visitor should have at least once.

Clinic Appointment Han River

A slow final morning in the city — a last browse of Korea's beauty stores, a good coffee. Then a short domestic flight to Jeju Island, arriving by evening.

K-beauty Shopping Fly to Jeju
Jeju Island Days 8–12

Check into your coastal resort and exhale. A gentle afternoon walk along Jeju's volcanic coastline. Evening spa at your hotel — the natural shift from active treatment days to restorative rest.

Jeju Coastline Resort Spa

Morning on Hallasan — Korea's highest peak and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Afternoon at the iconic Seongsan Ilchulbong, the volcanic crater that rises from the sea at sunrise.

Hallasan Seongsan Ilchulbong

Volcanic spring bath in the morning — Jeju's mineral-rich water is genuinely good for skin. A traditional Korean jjimjilbang sauna, then a slow afternoon on one of Jeju's turquoise-water beaches.

Volcanic Spring Bath Jjimjilbang Sauna Jeju Beach

Watch the legendary haenyeo — Jeju's famed women free-divers — demonstrate their ancient craft. Afternoon inside Manjanggul, one of the world's longest lava tube caves. Seafood dinner by the harbor.

Haenyeo Divers Manjanggul Lava Tube

A slow breakfast with ocean views. Your concierge delivers your post-treatment home-care routine — a personalized skincare plan to maintain your results once you're home. Depart from Jeju International Airport.

Home-care Consultation Departure
